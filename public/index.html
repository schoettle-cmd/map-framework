Protected by California Cottage Food Law

All sellers on Cottage operate under California's AB 1616 Cottage Food law. This means every kitchen is registered with their local county, follows food safety guidelines, and is legally permitted to sell homemade goods directly to you.

Class A permits allow direct-to-consumer sales up to $75,000/year. Class B permits allow indirect sales through stores and restaurants up to $150,000/year.
